Dyslexia Screening Quiz

1. Do you often lose your place while reading? 🔊

2. Do you find it hard to break words into syllables when reading? 🔊

3. Do you confuse letters like "b" and "d" or "p" and "q"? 🔊

4. Do you struggle with reading maps or graphs? 🔊

5. Do you struggle with basic math like counting or telling time? 🔊

6. Do numbers often get reversed or appear in the wrong order? 🔊

7. Do words blur or move around when you read? 🔊

8. Do you get headaches or tired eyes when reading for a long time? 🔊

9. Do you struggle to recall instructions shortly after hearing them? 🔊

10. Do you forget what you read even after reading it carefully? 🔊
