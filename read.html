Reading Assessment

How to Read the Passage:

  1. Click the "Start Reading" button when you're ready
  2. Read the passage below out loud at your normal pace
  3. Speak clearly into your microphone
  4. Click "Stop" when you finish reading
  5. Review what you've read and click "Submit"

Take your time and read at a comfortable pace. There is no rush!

The sun was setting behind the mountains, casting long shadows across the valley. Birds were flying back to their nests, and the cool evening breeze rustled through the trees. A small river flowed gently through the middle of the green meadow, reflecting the golden sunlight. Children were playing in the distance, their laughter echoing across the peaceful landscape. It was a perfect end to a beautiful summer day.
